    National Corporate Innovation Index (NCII)
    The National Corporate Innovation Index is aimed to steer and accelerate innovation within Public Listed Companies (PLCs) and Government Linked Companies (GLCs) in Malaysia. As the future growth of PLCs and GLCs depends on their levels of innovation, the index will help identify innovation activities that will ensure their long-term sustainability. Being major drivers of the Malaysian economy, PLCs and GLCs also play a significant role in stimulating innovation in the Malaysian ecosystem. Therefore, the resulting increase in innovation outputs will directly impact Malaysia's economy, propelling Malaysia towards becoming an innovative nation by 2020. In addition, Malaysia's ranking in the international scene (e.g. INSEAD, Global Innovation Index, WEF Global Competitiveness Report) will also be improved. The National Corporate Innovation Index, a project under purview of Agensi lnovasi Malaysia, is one of the initiatives within the National Innovation Strategy.
