1. Innovation Strategy
This segment captures the strategic importance of innovation in your organization and why you are innovating. Secondly, what type of innovation is important & how you choose to manage your intellectual property. The worse thing to provide your team when it comes to innovation is a blank piece of paper. Don't ask them to do whatever they want. When it comes to innovation, you need to be specific. Organisations that align their strategy initiatives with other efforts are more likely to see value being generated from innovation. Leaders, who then communicate a clear direction for innovation and provide specific focus and expected outcomes, will acquire greater returns on innovation.
